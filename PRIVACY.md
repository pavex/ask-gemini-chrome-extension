# Privacy Policy for Gemini Quick Ask

**Last Updated: June 17, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Gemini Quick Ask** (the "Extension") handles user data. We take your privacy very seriously and follow a "Privacy by Design" approach.

## 1. Data Collection and Transmission
- **No Personal Data Collection:** The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to the developer or any third-party servers.
- **Direct API Communication:** The Extension communicates exclusively with the official Google Gemini API (`https://generativelanguage.googleapis.com`) to provide the AI response service. The content you send (selections and prompts) is sent directly to Google.
- **No Tracking:** We do not use any tracking pixels, analytics tools, or cookies to monitor user behavior.

## 2. Data Storage
- **Local Storage Only:** Your Gemini API Key, custom prompts, system instructions, and UI settings are stored **strictly in your browser's local storage** (`chrome.storage.local`).
- **Encryption:** Data stored locally is subject to the security measures provided by your browser and operating system.
- **No Cloud Storage:** We do not provide or use any cloud synchronization services for your settings or keys.

## 3. Third-Party Services
- **Google Gemini API:** By using this Extension, you interact with Google services. Your use of the Gemini API is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [Google's Terms of Service](https://policies.google.com/terms).
- **No Other Third Parties:** We do not share, sell, or trade your data with any other third-party companies or individuals.

## 4. Permissions Usage
- **Storage:** Used to save your API Key and settings locally.
- **Scripting & ActiveTab:** Used to read the text of the page you are currently visiting only when you trigger the Extension.
- **ContextMenus:** Used to add the "Ask Gemini" option to your right-click menu.

## 5. User Control
- You can delete all data stored by the Extension at any time by uninstalling the Extension or clearing your browser's extension data.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the official GitHub repository.
